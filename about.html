Hello

My name is Fabio Tricarico.

I have had a passion for video games and the gaming industry since I was a child, and have garnered an interest in gaming journalism since my teenage years.

More info on myself would be that I grew up in the Edgemead suburb of Cape Town and attended Edgemead High School.

After finishing high school, I decided to join the workforce and began working at Global Load Control as a Load Controller dealing with short and long-distance commercial flights.

I then decided to get my degree in Psychology and English at Pearsons Institution of Higher Education, which I completed cum laude in 2021.

I feel the passion I have for gaming and the skills I acquired due to my English degree have made me apt in researching and writing informative as well as well-constructed news articles.

Furthermore, the constant growth and change within the industry means that informative journalism is essential as individuals wish to be updated on the latest developments in the ever-growing industry.

Additional Information

At a young age I spent 2 years in Switzerland where my father was raised which has given me a certain competency in speaking German.

Additional Informatione

In addition to video games, my hobbies include reading and music. I enjoy reading which influenced my decision to study literature and my passion for music has led to me learning to play the guitar.
